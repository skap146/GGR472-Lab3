Analyzing Crime in Toronto

This map looks at 2024 crime data in the city of Toronto. You can filter by crime type and/or the month the crimes occurred. Click on a crime point (a black point) to learn more about it. You'll also notice that Toronto is split up into wards on the map. You can classify wards by education, employment, or youth. Have fun exploring!
